##############################
# Cucumber + Cuke4Duke HowTo #
##############################

How to run acceptance tests:
============================

1. Prepare Maven
----------------

Execute

mvn -Dcucumber.installGems=true -P cuke4duke integration-test 

to install the cucumber gems for ruby. 

You may need the following repositories:

<repositories>
	<repository>
    	<id>codehaus</id>
		<url>http://repository.codehaus.org</url>
	</repository>
	<repository>
		<id>cukes</id>
		<url>http://cukes.info/maven</url>
	</repository>
</repositories>

<pluginRepositories>
	<pluginRepository>
		<id>cukes</id>
		<url>http://cukes.info/maven</url>
	</pluginRepository>
</pluginRepositories>

 If you are behind a proxy, you should setup the proxy connection in your ~/.m2/settings.xml

<proxies>
	<proxy>
		<id>blue</id>
		<active>true</active>
		<protocol>http</protocol>
		<host>some-proxy</host>
		<port>some-port</port>
		<username>xxx</username>
		<password>xxx</password>
		<nonProxyHosts>...</nonProxyHosts>
	</proxy>
</proxies>
 
2. Execute the test
-------------------

Execute:

mvn -P cuke4duke compile integration-test

You may do this in the ulrice module or the ulrice-sample module.

After this have a look at: ulrice-sample/target/features_result.html or ulrice-sample/target/features_result.pdf

3. Understand it
----------------

Have a look at the feature file:

ulrice-sample/features/behavior/behavior.feature

And then the step definitions:

net.ulrice.sample.module.behavior.BehaviorSteps
